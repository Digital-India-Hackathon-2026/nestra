## Idea
NESTRA is an AI-powered smart pregnancy monitoring system that continuously monitors maternal health using wearable sensors. It tracks heart rate, SpO₂, body temperature, fall detection, and baby movement, sends emergency SOS alerts with location to caregivers and hospitals, and provides AI-based health insights for early risk detection. The solution aims to reduce maternal and fetal health risks, especially in rural and remote areas, by enabling affordable, real-time monitoring and faster emergency response.
